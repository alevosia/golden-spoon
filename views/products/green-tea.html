Green Tea

Our green tea is made from the leaves of Camellia Sinensis that undergone minimal oxidation during processing. Green tea is the healthiest beverage on the planet. It is loaded with antioxidants and nutrients that have powerful effects on the body.

HOW TO PREPARE

Place one teabag of Golden Spoon Green Tea into cup.

Pour fresh boiling water and leave to infuse for 1-2 minutes.

Add sugar or lemon if desired for extra taste.
