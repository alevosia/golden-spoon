Lagundi

is a large shrub native to the Philippines that has been used as a traditional herbal medicine for centuries. It is commonly found in tropical, subtropical and warm temperate regions throughout the world, especially in the Philippines.

HOW TO PREPARE

Place one teabag of Golden Spoon Lagundi Herbal Tea into cup.

Pour fresh boiling water and leave to infuse for 1-2 minutes.

Add sugar or lemon if desired for extra taste.

HEALTH BENEFITS

  • Relieves asthma and pharyngitis
  • Recommended relief of rheumatism, dyspepsia, boils, diarrhea
  • Helps in treatment of cough, colds, fever and flu and other bronco pulmonary disorders
  • Alleviates symptoms of Chicken Pox
  • Helps in removal of worms and boils
