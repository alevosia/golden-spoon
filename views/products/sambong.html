Sambong

also known as “Blumea balsamifera”, or “Blumea camphor”, is an aromatic shrub that grows from one to four meters in height. It is a shrub that grows wild in the tropical climate countries such as Philippines, as well as India, Africa and even in eastern Himalayas."

HOW TO PREPARE

Place one teabag of Golden Spoon Sambong Tea into cup.

Pour fresh boiling water and leave to infuse for 1-2 minutes.

Add sugar or lemon if desired for extra taste.

HEALTH BENEFITS

  • Its leaves can be used as a poultice for abscesses
  • Decoction of roots and leaves for fevers, kidney stones and cystitis
  • Decoction of leaves used to induce dieresis for purpose of treating kidney stones
  • Sitz-bath of boiled leaves helps in relieving rheumatic pains of waist and back
  • Used in treating respiratory tract affections like sinusitis, asthmatic, bronchitis and influenza
